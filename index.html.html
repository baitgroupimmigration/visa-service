FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
How long does processing take?
Normally we process invitation letters within 3 business days and send them via email. Express same-day processing is also available at extra charge.
Do you assist with the visa application itself?
Yes, we assist with visa applications for countries that offer e-visa or online application options. You may still need to visit the embassy or VFS for biometric data if required.
What documents do I need to submit?
You will need to submit your full name (exactly as in your passport), a scanned copy of your passport, and one personal photo. Additional documents may be required depending on the country.
Is express processing available?
Yes, express same-day processing is available for urgent cases. Additional charges apply. Please contact us directly via WhatsApp.
Are payments refundable?
All payments are non-refundable. We strongly recommend ensuring all your details are correct before submitting your application.
